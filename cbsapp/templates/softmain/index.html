Who Are We?

Softsearch is a software, mobile and web applications provider that offers custom development services. We are a team of enthusiastic and highly motivated professional software, web and mobile application designers and developers. We thrive on a technical challenge, but we also relish the buzz provided by a team-working environment. We're also experienced business professionals who'll understand your business requirements.

We build Bespoke systems for a range of businesses, from SME’s to large organizations. We are committed to providing scalable and secure software solutions, delivering excellent customer service and constantly exceeding customer expectations in agile software development.
